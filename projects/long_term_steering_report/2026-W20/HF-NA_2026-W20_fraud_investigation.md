**Overall:** Fraud Approval Rate (FAR) improved from 90.59% to 92.19% (+1.77% relative, +1.60pp) in 2026-W20, reaching the highest level in the 8-week analysis period.

| Duplicate Rate | Increased across all segments | +2.86pp regional | ⚠️ |

**Key Findings:**
- US drove the majority of FAR improvement with +2.31% change, accounting for 75% of total volume (17,352 customers)
- Duplicate Rate increased significantly across all segments: regional +2.86pp, US +13.59% relative, CA +10.74% relative, Referral channel +19.91% relative
- PF Block Rate decreased from 2.04% to 1.31% (-0.73pp), contributing to higher approval rates
- Duplicate Block Rate increased slightly from 5.08% to 5.51% (+0.43pp) but did not offset FAR gains
- Volume decreased by 5.0% (24,220 → 23,008 customers) week-over-week

**Action:** Monitor — The FAR improvement is positive and no segments exceed alert thresholds. However, the rising Duplicate Rate trend across all segments (particularly Referral at +19.91%) warrants continued observation to ensure it does not indicate emerging fraud patterns or system issues.
