**Key Findings:**
- FAR increase of +2.31pp represents a recovery from W19's dip (-1.87pp), bringing the rate back in line with W18 levels (92.14%)
- PF Block Rate decreased significantly from 2.27% to 1.03% (-1.24pp), directly contributing to higher approval rates

**Action:** Monitor — The FAR improvement is driven by reduced PF blocking and appears to be a normalization after W19's dip. Continue monitoring the rising Duplicate Rate trend, particularly in Referral channel.
